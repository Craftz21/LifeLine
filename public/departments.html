Departments

At LifeLine Hospital, we offer a wide range of specialized departments to ensure that you receive the best care possible. Our expert teams are dedicated to improving the health and well-being of our patients. Below is a list of the major departments:

  • Cardiology - Focuses on diagnosing and treating heart conditions, including coronary artery disease, heart failure, and arrhythmias.
  • Neurology - Specializes in the treatment of disorders of the nervous system, including brain and spinal cord conditions.
  • Orthopedics - Dedicated to the diagnosis and treatment of bone, joint, and soft tissue disorders, including fractures, arthritis, and sports injuries.
  • Pediatrics - Focuses on the medical care of infants, children, and adolescents, including preventive care and treatment of common illnesses.
  • Oncology - Specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of various types of cancer using advanced therapies and personalized care plans.
  • Radiology - Uses imaging technology such as X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans to diagnose and monitor medical conditions.
